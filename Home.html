One platform for all your health technology needs

Our solution brings together the management of all physical and software based medical devices to improve efficiency, provide greater visibility for data governance and help mange cyber risk. We enable greater collaboration with teams that have remained siloed to date.

Hospitals are facing increasing challenges in the management of digital health technologies

With connectivity for med devices came great opportunities for improved patient safety, outcomes and efficiencies yet it has caused heightened complexity in the management of hospital networks, increased risk from cyber security incidents and data breaches and added complexity with compatibility and the management of patching, updates and maintenance requirements

Cyber risk coupled with the proliferation of SaMD and AIaMD have brought about an overhaul of the regulatory framework adding additional administrative burden to overstretched hospitals Data Management and Governance

Siloes: previously one team dealing with devices, now the responsibility lies with various siloed teams that have not traditionally worked together
