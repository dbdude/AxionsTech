Software Overview
We are building a series of modular packages aimed at managing all aspects of medical devices from. Commisioning, through lifecycle management and disposal.

Overview

Designed to cater the needs of the entire clinical infrastructure and built to comply with proposed new standards. There are currently 19 identified components which are capable of running individually or combined to provide more functionality as needed.

There are some key design elements of our proposal. We are intending to address all requirements of medical science teams from acquisition, usage through to disposal. Most of these requirements have come directly from these teams and now its time to build it collaboration with a number of hospital partners.

Recent advances in ML/AI can be leveraged to assist in the medical device world. This will provide significant benefit. It is important that we are proposing some novel implementations and not shoehorning an existing 'round' solution into a clinical 'square' hole. One example is we intend to semi-automate device failure reporting. If we just added a chat-bot it is likely that it will not be used because the ward staff cannot spare the time. Asking a single question however could reduce false reports and not be very onerous on the ward team.

Management and Governance

We will provide an overview and details analysis on all aspects of clinical devices. Regulatory reporting is built in along with analysis of the effectiveness of the current PPM schedules from a safety and efficiency perspective.
